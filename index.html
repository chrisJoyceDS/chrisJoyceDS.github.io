Identifying GPU Models Across Subreddits

Utilizing Pushshift and a diverse suite of Classification MachineLearning Models, identify a given reddit post between two subreddits, and find whether or not a specific GPU Model is mentioned. Using 400k individual posts from r/NVIDIA and r/AMD, accurately classify the subreddit origin and capture any mention of Intel GPU Models for marketing outreach analysis.

What matters more when predicting housing prices, the measurement of actual value (sqft) or the catogization depreciating facets? Using Test of Significance, compare the Root Mean Squared Error and R-Squared values of models trained on squarefeet measurements v. ordinal categorical data.
